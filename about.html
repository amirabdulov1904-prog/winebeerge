About Wine & Beer Georgia

Wine & Beer Georgia is a leading export partner for Georgian alcoholic beverages. We work with 40+ factories across all segments — from economy table wines to premium aged cognacs and natural chacha. We are not middlemen — we are official representatives of Georgian producers, helping international buyers find the right partner, negotiate the best price, and execute the deal end-to-end.
